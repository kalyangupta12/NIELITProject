About Assam State Higher Education Admission Portal- 2023


The Assam State Higher Education Admission Portal - 2023 is being introduced for admission into all UG Programmes in Assam State Universities for academic session 2023-24 under the Department of Higher Education Assam. The Assam State Higher Education Admission Portal will provide a common platform and equal opportunities to candidates across the state, especially those from rural and other remote areas and help establish better connect with the Universities. A single portal will enable the Candidates to cover a wide outreach and be part of the admissions process to Assam State Universities.
